Did you know?

A running tap wastes 6 liters of water per minute. Turning it off while brushing teeth saves 8,000 liters a year!

Source: Waterwise / WHO

Non-Revenue Water (NRW) Reduction

Target: Reduce national NRW to 25% by 2030 (Source: 12MP)

Current Avg (~35%) Source: SPAN 2024 Target (25%)

Replacing aging asbestos-cement pipes with HDPE and implementing smart pressure management systems to minimize physical losses.

Alternative Sources

Reducing river dependency by exploring groundwater, off-river storage (ORS), and large-scale rainwater harvesting.

Tariff Reform

Implementing fair Tariff Setting Mechanisms (TSM) to enable water operators to fund continuous infrastructure upgrades.

Polluter Pays Principle

Strict enforcement of environmental laws. Industrial polluters must bear the full cost of remediation, protecting our raw water sources from contamination events.
