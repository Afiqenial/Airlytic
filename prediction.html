For Decision Makers (Strategic Planning)

Financial Strategy

Budgeting vs. Infrastructure

Evidence: Divergence between Linear (Blue) & Ensemble (Red).

Use the Red Line for OpEx/Revenue budgeting (accurate short-term), but build infrastructure for the Blue Line (safety margin).

Ref: Budgeting Framework
Digital Transformation

East Malaysia Data Gap

Evidence: AI imputation needed for 2023-2024.

Data completeness is a risk. Invest in Digital Telemetry & IoT Meters to eliminate the need for imputation in future dashboards.

Ref: National Data Standards
Risk Management

Managing Volatility

Evidence: Hybrid Model (Orange) "Zig-Zag" patterns.

Maintain a 10-15% reserve margin ("Headroom") for random spikes. Accelerate Inter-State Transfers to balance regional instability.

Ref: Risk Mitigation
Conservation

Targeted Demand Mgmt

Evidence: Lower ARIMA trends vs High Linear trends.

Efficiency improvements are working. Double down on conservation to keep actual usage closer to the lower curve and defer CAPEX.

Ref: Demand Side Mgmt
Decision Makers

The "Virtual Dam" Strategy

Evidence: Gap between Blue (Linear) & Purple (ARIMA) lines.

Treat NRW reduction as a "New Plant". Instead of RM 500M for a new dam, allocate RM 200M to pipe replacement to force demand down to the Purple Line.

Ref: RMK-12 (25% NRW Target)
State Operators

Dynamic "Peak-Hour" Tariffs

Evidence: Hybrid Model (Orange) volatility spikes.

Building for the single highest spike is inefficient. Implement Time-of-Use (TOU) tariffs for industries to flatten the peak during high-stress periods.

Ref: SPAN TSM Framework
Engineering Teams

Predictive Maintenance

Evidence: Seasonality in ARIMA/Hybrid models.

Use ARIMA troughs for maintenance scheduling. Never shut down when the Hybrid model predicts a spike in the next 30 days.

Ref: ISO 55000 Asset Mgmt
Government

Inter-State Equity Policy

Evidence: Steep Linear growth in some states vs. flat in others.

Use the dashboard to justify Raw Water Transfers (e.g., Sungai Perak to Penang) by showing the recipient's unsustainable "Blue Line" trajectory.

Ref: NAWABS / DID
IT & Data Teams

From "Gap Fill" to "Digital Twin"

Evidence: "Gap Fill" (Green Line) proves manual reporting lag.

Upgrade to a Digital Twin by feeding live SCADA data into the XGBoost model to re-forecast hourly. Move from rearview mirror to real-time simulation.

Ref: WST 2040 (Smart Grid)
